Background

I'm a researcher and engineer with a rare combination of 12+ years of industry software experience and a deep pivot into computational neuroscience and AI.

Currently pursuing my MSc at the Bernstein Center for Computational Neuroscience (BCCN) in Berlin, where I work on gaze prediction in dynamic scenes using large multimodal models.

My engineering roots span Java backend development, SaaS banking systems, and Agile leadership as a Senior Scrum Master. I bring a production mindset to research — building robust, reproducible pipelines.
